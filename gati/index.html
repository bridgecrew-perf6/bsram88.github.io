Gender Advancement for Transforming Institutions (GATI) - An initiative from DST, India

The goal is to define ways to improve representation, career progression, & working environment for EVERYONE

More information

GATI at IITM

  • IIT Madras - one of the 30 GATI Charter Institutions in the GATI pilot initiative of DST.
  • Mandate - to improve gender parity in S & T at institutional level
  • Standard framework to evaluate
  • Articulation of policies for gender equality
  • Partnered with Queen Mary University of London for guidance & mentoring

"The program is for women in academic roles in STEM - faculty, staff, scholars, students"
